What is a temperature blanket?

Temperature blankets are knitted or crocheted blankets that use weather data to plot out the colors used in the blanket. Generally, the blankets use a year's worth of temperature data. The colors can be arranged in any regular pattern such as rows or squares. This example is a bias blanket with two rows for each day, one corresponding to the maximum temperature and the other to the minimum temperature. The locations here correspond to the ten largest cities by population in the US as well as DC and a couple locations that I wanted to try out for myself.
